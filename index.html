About Me

I build the parts of a data platform that are supposed to be invisible when they work: pipelines that don't quietly drop records, dashboards people actually trust, and the recovery logic that kicks in at 2 a.m. so nobody has to. Right now that's at Thoughtworks, working on data reliability, governance, and Power BI tooling wired up to LLM-based agents.

Off the clock, I've cleared 800+ LeetCode problems, placed AIR 56 in the National Engineering Olympiad, and co-authored two IEEE papers on deep learning.
